Our Story In Three Sentences

MobiClocks was developed out of necessity upon request by a construction executive after trying many different time and attendance systems that did not work as promised.

They experienced a range of problems from poor quality devices with bad or no connectivity to software with limited functionality for the way they did business.

Their goal was to build a system to combat labor fraud and increase accuracy by using true Face Recognition technology, and additionally one which was durable, mobile and easy to use.

Face Recognition

Face recognition technology provides much more accuracy than other biometric systems, or fob or RFID card-based systems. With face recognition you can only have one face per employee. Compare this to a fingerprint biometric system where each employee has ten fingers. With dishonest employees each finger can be registered as a different employee name. This creates the ghost employee scenario where several paychecks can be generated for one person. Also, fingerprint systems are very problematic for workers whose hands get dirty. They often fail to read the print and the worker has to use a pin number. Pin numbers are easily shared with friends who can use them to buddy punch for others.

The same is true for key fobs or RFID cards. They are easily passed off to others for buddy punching. A little dishonesty everyday can cost a business owner a lot of money every year. Shocking? Yes. And we couldn’t agree more.That’s why the best solution is MobiClocks time and attendance system with face recognition technology which identifies and verifies your employees at the point of punch within 3 seconds or less.

Geo-Fence & GPS Tracking

MobiClocks uses multiple Geo-Technologies including Geo-Fence, Geo-Location Stamps, and optional GPS Tracking features based on your business needs. There are two types of Geo-Fence shapes to choose from in MobiClocks.

One is a radial shape, the other is a customizable shape to fit the boundary needs of your job site. A Geo-Stamp is taken at the time of every punch, and if selected the optional GPs tracking can determine the job sites visited by your workers while they are punched in.

Flag Alert System

MobiClocks time and attendance system comes with an extensive flag alert system which detects abnormalities or problem punches. The alerts are displayed on the main dashboard screen and are clearly visible and color coded.

Flag Categories include missed punch, suspicious punch, offline punch and failed punch attempt to name a few, but there are others. You decide which flags you want to see on your dashboard and easily hide the ones you don’t need
